### Proven WebSearch Query Format

**Success Factors**:
1. Full title in quotes ensures precision
2. First author reduces ambiguity
3. Year filters duplicates
4. "cgspace" helps find CGIAR repository copies
5. Explicit "DOI abstract keywords" targets metadata
